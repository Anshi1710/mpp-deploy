Introducing Mahavir Polyplast Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 2008, Mahavir Polyplast Pvt. Ltd. stands as a distinguished player in the field of PVC pipeline manufacturing. With registration in the IAF and ISO 9001 certification, we take pride in our commitment to quality and excellence. Under the dynamic leadership of Mr. Ramnivas Gupta, the Managing Director, our company has thrived and expanded its footprint in the industry.

We are known for our construction and infrastructure sectors. Some of our key products include:

  • PVC Pipes (20 MM to 314)
  • Casing Pipe
  • CPVC Pipe
  • Column Pipe
  • UPVC Pipe
  • HDPE Sprinkler Pipe
  • Garden Pipe
  • Conduit Electrical Pipe
  • India Mark Hand Pump Machine
  • India Mark Sariya
